Bayt 'Ur al-Fauqa (Bethoron Superior)

Also known as Bethoron Superior, Vetus Bethor

Tower or smaller fortified site West Bank Beit Ur al-Fauqa, West Bank

Bayt 'Ur al-Fauqa — biblical Upper Beth Horon — stood on the ancient pass from the coastal plain up to Jerusalem. A Frankish tower known simply as al-Burj ('the tower') marked the Crusader presence there; the village was granted to the Holy Sepulchre in the twelfth century.
