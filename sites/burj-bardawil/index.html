Burj Bardawil

Also known as Burj el-Bardawil, al-Baubariya

Tower or smaller fortified site West Bank Burj Bardawil, West Bank

Burj Bardawil — 'Baldwin's Tower' — is a small semi-fortified courtyard building in the central West Bank hills, with projecting turrets and barrel-vaulted ranges. Its name links it in popular memory to one of the first Crusader kings of Jerusalem, though the precise building history is uncertain.
