Church of Saint George (Lod / Lydda)

Church or religious site Israel Church of Saint George (Lod / Lydda)

Grand 12th-century Crusader church over the symbolic tomb of Saint George at Lydda, a major pilgrimage site and the seat of the Bishop of Lydda. Razed by Saladin's armies in 1191; a Mamluk mosque was raised over the ruins in 1268, and a Greek Orthodox church was rebuilt on the eastern end of the Crusader nave and northern aisle in the 19th century, sharing the site with the mosque.
