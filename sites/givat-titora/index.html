Givat Titora

Also known as Qalaat al-Tantura, Qul'at al-Tantura, Titora Hill

Major castle / fortress Israel Givat Titora, Modi'in, Israel

A small castle or road fortress above the Modi'in plain, known in Arabic as Qal'at al-Tantura and in Hebrew as Tel Titora. It likely guarded the road from the coastal plain up to Jerusalem. The masonry remains are modest but occupy a prominent hill now in the middle of modern Modi'in-Maccabim-Re'ut.
