Jabal Quruntul (Mount of Temptation)

Also known as Jabal ad-Duqq, Tahunat al-Hawa, Doctis, castellum Abrahamti

Major castle / fortress West Bank Mount of Temptation, Jericho, West Bank

A Templar castle established on the summit of Jabal Quruntul, the traditional Mount of Temptation above Jericho, reusing natural caves and cisterns. The site is now best known for the cliff-clinging Greek Orthodox Monastery of the Temptation, which was built centuries later on and around the earlier remains.
